Find Out More About Us

Our Organization is established under a routine college project of BCA VI Sem 2018-21

Our firm act as a middle men..

Our firm takes the food from various food outlets, these food items which gets wasted usually are supplied to hungry peoples, and our Volunteer take the food for the needy person. Our firm also sometimes buys food for critical areas especially during this pandemic.
